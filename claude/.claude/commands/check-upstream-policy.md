Report:
1. **Policy found?** Yes/No, with location and a brief summary of the stance (permissive, cautious, restrictive).
2. **Disclosure requirements**: What attribution format does the project expect (e.g., `Assisted-by:`, `Generated by:`, specific trailer format)?
3. **Restrictions**: Anything explicitly prohibited (e.g., AI review comments, agentic submissions, mass PRs)?
4. **DCO/CLA**: Does the project require sign-off or a CLA? Any specific notes about AI and CLA compliance?
5. **No policy?** If nothing is found, note that and recommend transparent disclosure as a default.
