# Chapka — agent context

Chapka is a starter kit for **open source** work in an **AI-first** workflow. Agents should align with permissive-with-guardrails norms: AI tools are fine; **humans** remain accountable for every contribution.

## Where to look

- **[cursor/README.md](cursor/README.md)** — how Cursor rules, this file, and templates fit together.
- **[ASSESSMENT_Claude.md](ASSESSMENT.md)** — landscape of community policies and practical guidance.
- **[cursor/CHECKLIST.md](cursor/CHECKLIST.md)** — pre-PR considerations (verification, disclosure, licensing).
- **Project rules** — [.cursor/rules/open-source-ai-core.mdc](.cursor/rules/open-source-ai-core.mdc), [.cursor/rules/open-source-ai-attribution.mdc](.cursor/rules/open-source-ai-attribution.mdc).

## Working in another repository

Always prefer the **upstream** project’s `CONTRIBUTING`, governance, and AI-specific policy over Chapka defaults. If they forbid or constrain something Chapka allows, follow upstream.
